About me

Juan Pablo Gamucero Arana | BSc Physics Student

I was born in August 13, 1998 in a small town called Santa Cruz de Juventino Rosas located in Guanajuato state, México. Currently, I’m an undergraduate physics student at Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México attending my last year of Bachelor’s. Since May 2021, I joined the program of social service Frontiers in precision cosmology: from alternative theories of gravity to cosmo-statistics with machine learning under the counseling of researcher Celia del Carmen Escamilla Rivera. I'm associate student at Instituto de Ciencias Nucleares - UNAM. Agregar que también doy clases particulares, participo en Animathica, y me está empezando a gustar la progamación competitiva.
